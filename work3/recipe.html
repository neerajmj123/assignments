Delicious Chocolate Chip Cookies

Preparation time 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter,softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanila extract
  • 2 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoons baking soada
  • 2 cup of chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degree F(175 degree C).
  2. In a large bowl ,cream together the butter and sugar until smooth
  3. Beat in the eggs one at time ,then stir in the vanila .
  4. In a separate bowl ,combine the flour and baking soda; gradualy add to the butter mixture.
  5. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  6. Drop by rounded tablespoon chips onto ungreased cookie sheets
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until edges are golden brown
